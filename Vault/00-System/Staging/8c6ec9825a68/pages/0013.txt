MAKING A DRAGON SHAMAN

Asadragon shaman, you primarily act as a melee combatant,
but your class features also allow you to grant benefits to those
fighting around you. In a standard group of adventurers, you
can stand in for the fighter or the cleric. Your combat prowess
isn't quite as great as a fighter’s, but you can employ special
attacks and special defenses a fighter can't access. Although
you lack many of the cleric’s specialized spells, you do possess
the ability to heal and remove negative conditions, Asa dragon
shaman, you can grow tough scales, breathe fire or another
type of energy, and soar on dragon wings—and that barely
scratches the surface of the powers at your command
