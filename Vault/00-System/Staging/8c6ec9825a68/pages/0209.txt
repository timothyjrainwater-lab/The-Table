SKILL PRIORITY LISTS

Each of the entries below is a list of skills, rated in order from
most important to least important for a typical member of
the class. Start at the beginning of your class's skill list and
count off the number of skills your character is entitled
to based on the information in Table A-2, The character
gains each of those skills at the maximum ranks allowed
for his level. A human character gains one bonus skill at
maximum ranks.

For example, if you're playing a halfore barbarian with
an Intelligence score of 8, you are entitled to 4—1 = 3 skills.
Take the first three skills from the barbarian list (Survival
Listen, and Jump), and spend the maximum ranks allowed
for each (character level + 3).
