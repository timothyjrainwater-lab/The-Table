Che Rajian Pantheon

The denizens of Sri Raji worship an extensive
pantheon of deities that are so complexly inter-
twined that outsiders often have difficulty grasping
the basic concepts of their religion. The core of
Rajian belief is the concept of reincarnation; Rajians
believe that the world is actually a kind of mortal
hell. Souls are doomed to live, die and be reborn as
new creatures in an endless cycle of suffering. Only
through perfect spiritual understanding and en-
lightenmentcan souls escape to an eternal paradise.

Two deities in the Rajian pantheon are par-
ticularly noteworthy. The Dark Sisters are cultists
of the goddess Kali. Each day, they collect a human
sacrifice, hauling the unfortunate to the leader of
their cult, Arijani. Also knownas the Black Mother,
Kali is a goddess of both destruction and creation.
She isa bloodthirsty entity who delights in murder,
but she also creates new life from death.
