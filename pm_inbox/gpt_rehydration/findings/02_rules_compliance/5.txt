### Core Architecture & Authority

**DEC-001:** The Box is the sole mechanical authority.

* Source: T1_README.md (architecture section)
* Rationale: Prevent LLM from influencing game mechanics.
* Consequences: Lens/Spark cannot mutate state; all mechanical outcomes originate in Box.

**DEC-002:** The Lens is a one-way membrane from Box to Spark.

* Source: T1_README.md
* Rationale: Preserve determinism and authority boundary.
* Consequences: No back-propagation of LLM output into combat state.

**DEC-003:** Spark never decides AC, damage, saves, or condition results.

* Source: T1_PROJECT_COHERENCE_DOCTRINE.md
* Rationale: Prevent nondeterministic mechanics.
* Consequences: Spark output must be validated and non-authoritative.

**DEC-004:** All game state transitions are event-sourced.

* Source: T1_README.md
* Rationale: Deterministic replay and auditability.
* Consequences: No hidden state mutation outside event log.

**DEC-005:** Determinism invariant: identical seed + inputs = identical state hash.

* Source: T1_PROJECT_COHERENCE_DOCTRINE.md
* Rationale: Replay validation and trust.
* Consequences: No clock/time/random calls in resolution path.

### Ruleset Scope

**DEC-006:** System implements D&D 3.5e only.

* Source: T1_AGENT_DEVELOPMENT_GUIDELINES.md §7
* Rationale: Prevent scope creep and rule contamination.
* Consequences: No 5e mechanics permitted.

**DEC-007:** No advantage/disadvantage mechanic.

* Source: T1_AGENT_DEVELOPMENT_GUIDELINES.md §7
* Rationale: 5e contamination risk.
* Consequences: All bonuses are numeric modifiers.

**DEC-008:** No proficiency bonus system.

* Source: T1_AGENT_DEVELOPMENT_GUIDELINES.md §7
* Rationale: 5e-only mechanic.
* Consequences: Skills follow 3.5 ranks + ability mod.

**DEC-009:** Concentration is a skill check (DC 10 + damage), not a spell slot limiter.

* Source: T1_AGENT_DEVELOPMENT_GUIDELINES.md §7
* Rationale: Distinguish 3.5 from 5e semantics.
* Consequences: Multiple “concentration duration” spells allowed concurrently.

**DEC-010:** 0-level spells consume slots (no at-will cantrips).

* Source: Guidelines contamination table
* Rationale: RAW compliance.
* Consequences: No infinite casting mechanic.

---

### Combat Engine

**DEC-011:** AoO implemented for movement (initial milestone).

* Source: T3_KNOWN_TECH_DEBT.md
* Rationale: Phased capability rollout.

**DEC-012:** Weapon range currently stubbed pending schema update.

* Source: T3_KNOWN_TECH_DEBT.md

**DEC-013:** Temporary modifiers integration deferred (stubbed).

**DEC-014:** Full AC/HP recompute blocked pending formal design.

* Source: T3_KNOWN_TECH_DEBT.md TD-007

### Spell System

**DEC-015:** Spell resolution pipeline separated into SpellResolver, DurationTracker, AoE Rasterizer.

* Source: T1_PROJECT_STATE_DIGEST.md
* Rationale: Maintain separation of concerns.
* Consequences: Modular validation possible.

**DEC-016:** Spell registry counts do not imply full RAW scenario validation.

* Source: Inferred from PSD test descriptions
* Rationale: Registry tests ≠ scenario correctness.
* Consequences: Requires scenario suite (future).

### Governance & Process

**DEC-017:** Known technical debt is tracked explicitly and not silently fixed.

* Source: T3_KNOWN_TECH_DEBT.md
* Rationale: Avoid scope creep.
* Consequences: Blocked items require formal dispatch.

**DEC-018:** Boundary laws (e.g., BL-017 no runtime UUID default) are enforced.

* Source: T1_AGENT_DEVELOPMENT_GUIDELINES.md
* Rationale: Determinism guarantee.
* Consequences: IDs injected externally.

**DEC-019:** Hardware-gated tests may skip but must not alter authoritative logic.

* Source: PSD (skipped tests note)
* Rationale: Local-first constraint.
* Consequences: Feature parity must degrade safely.

**DEC-020:** No silent guessing on missing data (fail-closed).

* Source: T1_PROJECT_COHERENCE_DOCTRINE.md
* Rationale: Deterministic integrity.
* Consequences: Exceptions preferred over fallback guesses.

# 2) Five Ambiguous / Contradictory / Missing SSoT Decisions

---

### A1 — “Cantrip” Terminology Drift

* Ambiguity: PSD uses “cantrips”; Guidelines forbid at-will semantics.
* Risk: Semantic contamination.
* Missing SSoT: Terminology enforcement clause.

---

### A2 — Concentration Semantics Boundary

* Ambiguity: DurationTracker references “concentration management” without explicitly prohibiting 5e-style exclusivity.
* Risk: Future implementer adds single-concentration limit.

---

### A3 — AC/HP Recompute Authority

* Ambiguity: Stub exists but no declared “authoritative recompute model” document.
* Risk: Parallel stacking logic in multiple modules.

---

### A4 — AoO Provocation Matrix Ownership

* Ambiguity: Movement-only implemented; provocation list not formally defined in docs.
* Risk: Inconsistent trigger expansion.

---

### A5 — Weapon Range Representation Standard

* Ambiguity: Range increment model not defined in schema contract doc.
* Risk: Divergent implementation interpretations.

# 3) Clarification Contracts (Exact Wording + Location)

---

## CLARIFICATION 1 — 0-Level Spell Terminology

**Wording:**

> “The system SHALL use the term ‘0-level spell’ in all authoritative documentation and code. The term ‘cantrip’ SHALL NOT imply at-will casting and SHALL NOT appear in mechanical logic.”

**Location:**
Append to `T1_AGENT_DEVELOPMENT_GUIDELINES.md` under §7 Contamination Risks.

---

## CLARIFICATION 2 — Concentration Scope

**Wording:**

> “Concentration in AIDM refers exclusively to the 3.5e skill check mechanic (DC 10 + damage or as specified). The system SHALL NOT enforce a global ‘one concentration spell at a time’ restriction.”

**Location:**
Add subsection under Spell System in `T1_PROJECT_COHERENCE_DOCTRINE.md`.

---

## CLARIFICATION 3 — AC/HP Authority

**Wording:**

> “AC and HP SHALL be recomputed exclusively through a single authoritative function within permanent_stats. No other module may apply stacking or recompute logic independently.”

**Location:**
New section in `T1_AGENT_DEVELOPMENT_GUIDELINES.md` under Derived Stats.

---

## CLARIFICATION 4 — AoO Provocation Matrix

**Wording:**

> “The list of provoking actions SHALL be explicitly enumerated in a single AoO Provocation Matrix document. All provocation checks SHALL reference this matrix.”

**Location:**
New doc `docs/MECHANICS_AOO_MATRIX.md`, referenced in Known Tech Debt.

---

## CLARIFICATION 5 — Weapon Range Standard

**Wording:**

> “Ranged weapons SHALL define: range increment (ft), maximum increments, and thrown/projectile category. Attack resolution SHALL compute penalties per increment and reject targets beyond maximum range.”

**Location:**
Weapon schema contract doc (append to T1_AGENT_DEVELOPMENT_GUIDELINES.md Combat Section).

---

# Summary

This Decision Log extracts enforceable intent already present in the project and surfaces 5 high-drift areas. The clarifications do not introduce new features; they formalize existing direction into enforceable contracts and single sources of truth to prevent mechanical and semantic drift.
