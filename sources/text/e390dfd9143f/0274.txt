Horses are widely domesticated for riding and as beasts of burden.

Combat
A horse not trained for war does not normally use its hooves to
attack. Its hoof attack is treated as a secondary attack and adds only
half the horse's Strength bonus to damage. (These secondary
attacks are noted with an asterisk in.the Attack and Full Attack
entries for the heavy horse and the light horse.)
