SALAMANDER SOCIETY
Flamebrothers, the smallest of the salamanders, are barbaric and
tribal, Often more sophisticated salamanders force their civiliza-
tion upon their smaller kin. Salamander nobility make a point of
|traveling through the planes, learning secrets to further their
power, These experienced creatures eventually return to master
their own kind and raise mighty kingdoms.

In a mixed society, status is determined by size and power—
flamebrothers are the lowest class and the front ranks of salaman-
der armies, Average salamanders are the middle class and the main
fighting force, while noble salamanders are commanders.
